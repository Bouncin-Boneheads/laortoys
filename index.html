LAOR TOYS LTD., established in 1995, is owned and managed
by Michal and Ilan Laor. LAOR produces the Original  ,
colorful plastic figurines sold by the millions throughout Europe and
called by many "the marbles of the new millennium." Original
  is a comprehensive concept which was created and
developed by LAOR TOYS and which is soon to be animated.
Original    are manufactured and packaged by LAOR TOYS
and manufacturing capability is one million Original    daily.
Original    merchandising products, which companies world
wide have been licensed to produce, range from linens to sneakers
to a compact disc which features the Original    theme song.
